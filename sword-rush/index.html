// SWING DIRECTIONALITY

Swing is the input.

Every attack is a continuous 360° vector. The direction you swing determines the launch angle, the damage cone, and the parry window your opponent has to mirror it. No combos. No strings. Just commitment.

Parry means swinging within 20° of the opposite vector inside a 100ms window. No damage. No durability cost. Pure skill expression.

⟵ try it: drag across the arena. faster drags = bigger hits.

// CORE RULES

No magic. Just swings.

01

Swing vector = launch vector.

The direction of your swing is the direction your opponent gets knocked. Swing up, they fly up. Swing across, they sail sideways. Physics does the rest.

02

Parry is mirror-within-20°.

Opposite the incoming swing inside a 100ms window. Clean parries cost nothing. Missed parries commit you to the wrong swing.

03

Weapons break.

Every swing chips durability. At zero, your weapon shatters — and every hero has a broken-weapon kit to survive without it.

04

FFA stocks, 3–4 players.

Last one standing takes the round. Ring-outs count. The arena shapes the fight — some maps pull, some push, some crumble.
