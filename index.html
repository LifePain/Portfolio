// CORE SYSTEMS

Command. Contest. Contain.

Every system tuned to make the horde feel alive, hungry, and relentless — and to give you the tools to hold against it.

Flow-Field Command

Thousands of units route simultaneously through integration fields. No stuck pathing, no A* bottlenecks — just armies that move like armies.

Evolving Horde AI

Xenos run an 8-state brain — hunt, forage, haul, defend, scout. Nests accumulate biomass, unlock tiers, and wake as global aggression climbs.

Build & Defend

Drop your HQ. Raise walls. Place turrets. Command selectable units with drag-box and right-click orders. Classic RTS control, modern scale.

Contest the Fauna

Share the map with territorial wildlife. Hunt them for biomass and traits — or deny xenos their food supply. Alpha guardians protect the richest dens.

Nest Cascade

Destroy a nest and its orphaned xenos go feral, inheriting DNA and fleeing to the next hive. Every victory reshapes the threat.

4-Player Co-Op

Separate bases. Shared vision. Resource trading between allies. No PvP — just you, your squad, and the swarm at the walls.
